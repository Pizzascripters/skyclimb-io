Skyclimb

How to Play:

Use your jetpack to scale the mountain while defending yourself from other players. The greater your elevation, the faster you accumulate gold, which you can spend at the 5 shops located around the map.

Controls:

W - Use Jetpack
A - Move Left
D - Move Right
123ZXC - Switch Item
Click - Use Item
Q - Drop Item
E - Enter Shop
F - Pickup Loot

Game Over
